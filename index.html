What is Computer Science?

Computer science basically deals with the study of methods for processing and organizing data in computers. It spans a wide range of interrelated areas from its theoretical and algorithmic foundations to cutting-edge developments in robotics, computer vision, intelligent systems, bioinformatics, and other exciting areas. It can as well be seen as the computers and computational systems which deals mostly with software systems, applications and theories. It is believed to be a rooted in mathematics. Computer Graphics

Majority of the early mainframe business computers produced outputs in form of punched cards, paper tape, or text printouts.However, system designers realized that some kinds of data were particularly amenable to a graphical representation. In the early 1950s, the first systems using the cathode ray tube (CRT) for graphics output found specialized application.For example,defense systems such as MIT Whirlwind and the Air Force’s SAGE used the CRT to display information such as the location of radar targets.

Networking

What is a Computer Network?

A computer network is a group of computer systems and other computing hardware devices that are linked together through communication channels to facilitate communication and resource-sharing among a wide range of users.

Uses Of Network

  1. File sharing
  2. Allows several users to share a single hardware device e.g. printer
  3. Allows for sharing of operating system or software on remote system
  4. Facilitate Communication via email, video conferencing, instant messaging e.t.c
  5. Easy access of information among users.
Types Of Network

Local Area Network(LAN): This is the interconnection of computers that are geographically close, i.e., in the same building.

Wide Area Network(WAN): This is the connection of computers which are largely apart via radio or line waves.

Metropolitan Area Network(MAN): It involves connecting computers within the same town or city.

Home Area Network(HAN): A network contained within a user's home that connects a person's digital devices.

Intranet: This is a network that is local to a company(resources can be retrieved by users within the company without having to go outside the company).This may be LANs, private WANs and MANs.

Extranet:This is an extended intranet, where certain internal services are made available to known external users or external business partners at remote locations.

Internet: This is when an unknown external users need to access internal resources in your network. In other words, your company might have a website that sells various products, and you want any external user to be able to access this service.

Virtual Private Network:This is a secured network type. A VPN is used to provide a secure connection across a public network, such as an internet. Extranets typically use a VPN to provide a secure connection between a company and its known external users or offices.

What are the qualities of a good network?
Authentication :is provided to validate the identities of the two peers.
Confidentiality: provides encryption of the data to keep it private from prying eyes.
Integrity :is used to ensure that the data sent between the two devices or sites has not been tampered with.
